# &#x09;				**CIA Triad**













* ###### **It is the core model in cybersecurity to protect data from unauthorized access, tampering, and downtime.”**





* ###### **It is a fundamental concept  that ensures information security through three core principles:**

###### 

1. ###### **Confidentiality**

###### 

###### **2. Integrity**

###### 

###### **3. Availability**

















##### **1.Confidentiality :-**



* ###### Ensures that data is accessible only to authorized users.

###### 

* ###### Protects sensitive information from unauthorized access.

###### 

* ###### Techniques/Tools: Encryption, passwords, access control, ACLs.

###### 

* ###### **Example:** Only HR staff can access employee salary data.







##### **2. Integrity :-**



* ###### Ensures that data remains accurate and unaltered.

###### 

* ###### Protects against unauthorized modification.

###### 

* ###### Techniques/Tools: Hashing, digital signatures, version control.

###### 

* ###### **Example:** Preventing tampering of financial transaction records.









##### **3. Availability :-**



* ###### Ensures that data and systems are accessible when needed

###### 

* ###### Protects against downtime or denial of service

###### 

* ###### Techniques/Tools: Backups, redundant systems, RAID, load balancers

###### 

* ###### **Example:** Company website remains accessible 24/7





###### Quick Summary Table :







###### Principle		Purpose				Example

###### 

###### Confidentiality		Keep data secret		Passwords, ACLs

###### Integrity		Keep data accurate		Hash, digital signature

###### Availability		Keep systems accessible		Backup, redundancy
